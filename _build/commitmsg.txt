Rarity system Phase A: gear rarities (color + % bonus) on drop

New Rarity plugin (loads after YEP_ItemCore, independent weapons/armors are on):
every weapon/armor obtained rolls one of six rarities -- Обычный/Необычный/
Особый/Редкий/Магический/Легендарный (weights 50/26/13/7/3/1). The item name is
tinted by rank (via item.textColor) and prefixed with the rank word; params are
boosted +5% per rank. Var87 is a floor-luck hook for deeper-better drops in a
later pass. Essences stay stackable for now (Phase C).

Needs in-game verification (rarity rolls at item-acquire runtime): start a game,
check starter gear / shop / chest drops show coloured rank names.
